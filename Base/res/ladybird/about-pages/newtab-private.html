Private Browsing

You are browsing in a private window. Ladybird keeps this session separate from your normal browsing.

In this session

  • Browsing history is not saved to your profile.
  • Cookies and site data use a separate private session.
  • When you close all private windows, that session data is discarded.

Please note

  • Downloads and bookmarks you create may still be saved.
  • Your network provider, employer, or the sites you visit may still see your activity.
  • Private browsing does not make you anonymous on the web.
